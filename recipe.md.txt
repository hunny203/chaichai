1. Start by taking a glass of water and put it in a tea pot.
2. Now add sugar as per taste and tea leaves to the water and let it boil for few minutes .
3. Add ginger or cinnamon as per your wish .
4. After the water starts to show reddish brown color , add milk to the water. 
5. Now let the ingredients boil , few times .
6. Your tasty tasty chai is taiyaar.
